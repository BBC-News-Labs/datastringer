Police statitstics parameters builder

Input your parameters

Start by filling the two dates, fromand to. A 6-month interval would do just fine.
Then use the map on the right-hand side to locate the area you want to get stats from. A click somewhere will give you the latitude and longitude of the place you clicked to. Input those in latitudeand longitude.
Note: The area selected will be a one-mile radius around the GPS coordinates.
