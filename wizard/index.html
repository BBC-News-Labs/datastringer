We're going to take you through the configuration of two examples, based on Police.uk data. Hopefully, you will be able to write your own stringers soon. We just want you to feel comfortable around here, and you to have templates to get inspiration from.

We've already put these examples together, so you won't be asked any coding, just to click on maps, don't worry.

Getting started

The two examples we selected are quite simple:

  • The first one will mash up crime statistics data for you, and ping you whenever there's a significant increasing or decreasing type of crimes in your neighbourhood,
  • The second will pay you an email every time there's a new police officer, or the local force schedules an event, or there's a change in the police priorities.

Go through this wizard. Take a look at our code. And then go read the wiki.
